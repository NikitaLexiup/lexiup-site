LexiUp is a vocabulary builder for German. It is not another course that demands your evening — it works in the background while you learn the language wherever you already learn it.

A few words a day. Six, nine or twelve — you choose the pace.
Delivered, not searched for. Words arrive as notifications and live on your home screen widget throughout the day.
A short test in the evening. It checks what actually stuck, then the next set begins.
Grammar where it helps. Short rule lessons for the alphabet, articles, pronouns, numbers and telling the time.
Works offline. Everything lives on your device; the cloud is only a backup.

What is LexiUp?
LU

A vocabulary builder for German. Not a course - you never have to open it.

A few words a day reach you as notifications and sit on your home screen widget. In the evening a two-minute test checks what stuck.

I already use another app for German.
LU

Good. Keep it.

I am not competing for your evening - I fill the gaps you already spend looking at your phone. Most people learning a language have a course, a teacher or a whole city speaking at them. What they lack is words.

How many words a day?
LU

Six, nine or twelve - you choose. You also pick the hours when I may reach you, and you can set a different window for each weekday.

What can I actually learn?
LU

Basics: the alphabet and its reading traps, der/die/das, pronouns across three cases, prepositions, numbers, telling the time, days and months.

A1, A2, B1: the most frequent German words, with verbs sorted by how often they really appear rather than by textbook order.

Each grammar rule is a short lesson, then the words that use it.

What do you know about me?
LU

Less than you would expect.

Your progress lives on your device; the cloud only holds a backup so a new phone does not cost you your history. No analytics, no profiling across other apps, nothing sold to anyone.

Notifications are scheduled on your phone, so I do not even learn when you study. And Profile → Delete account erases everything in one tap.
